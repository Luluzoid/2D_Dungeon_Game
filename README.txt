Goal: collect all 4 spheres in order to win!
Avoid bumping into the enemies to not lose health and die!


"An interactive element that aids the player" - a health potion that gives +1 health to the player
"An interactive element that hinders the player" - enemies that follow the player
"A victory condition" - collecting all 4 spheres scattered around the map
"A defeat condition" - losing all lives to the enemies


Reach goal implemented - Power-Up: 
There is a speed boost on the map that, when picked up, gives extra speed to the player for 5 seconds.
To know if the boost is active, an icon of the boost will appear in top right of the screen.
When the boost ends, the icon will disappear and player's speed will return to normal.
